About Me

I'm a creative designer with 6+ years of experience in creating beautiful and functional designs.

Based in New York, I specialize in UI/UX design, branding, and web design. I've collaborated with startups and established companies alike to create designs that not only look good but also solve real problems.

My design approach is centered around the user's needs and creating solutions that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional. I believe that great design should tell a story and create meaningful connections.
